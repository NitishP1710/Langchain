Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport for two teams of eleven players,
 played on a field with a central pitch and wickets at each end.
  One team bats to score runs, while the other fields to stop them 
  and dismiss batters. The objective is to score more runs than the
   opposing team to win the match. Originating in England, it is now
    a popular global sport
, particularly in Commonwealth countries like Australia, India, Pakistan, and South Africa
